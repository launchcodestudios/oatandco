WHY OATS?
After running a successful bakery & cupcake shop for 8 years, I decided I wanted to focus on creating delicious, gluten-free treats that everyone can enjoy. Oats were the perfect grain to start with. Oats are delicious, versatile and offer many health benefits that regular wheat flour can’t provide. Oat & Co. creates gourmet no bakes, rice crispie treats, edible cookie dough and more! We can’t wait to introduce you to how delicious gluten-free can be.
All of our gourmet no-bakes cookies are made with gluten-free oats and wheat free ingredients
